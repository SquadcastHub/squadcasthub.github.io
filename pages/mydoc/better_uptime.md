[Better Uptime](https://betterstack.com/better-uptime) is an uptime monitoring tool with a radically better infrastructure monitoring platform.

Route detailed alerts from Better Uptime to the right users in Squadcast.

## Using Better Uptime as an Alert Source

**(1)** From the navigation bar on the left, select **Services**. Pick the applicable **Team** from the Team-picker on the top. Next, click on **Alert Sources** for the applicable Service

![](images/alert_source_1.png)

**(2)** Search for **Better Uptime** from the Alert Source drop-down and copy the Webhook URL

![](images/better_uptime_1.png)

{{site.data.alerts.yellow-note-i}}
<b>Important</b><br/><br/>
<p>For an Alert Source to turn active (indicated by a <b>green dot - Receiving alerts</b> against the name of the Alert Source in the drop-down), you can either generate a test alert or wait for a real-time alert to be generated by the Alert Source.</p>
<p>An Alert Source is active if there is a recorded incident via that Alert Source for the Service in the last 30 days.</p>
{{site.data.alerts.end}}

## Create a Squadcast Webhook Alert in Better Uptime

**(1)** Login to your Better Uptime dashboard. Navigate to the **Integrations** section and then click on **Exporting data**

![](images/better_uptime_2.png)

**(2)** Under the **Exporting data** tab, select **Configure webhooks** and click on **Configure**

![](images/better_uptime_3.png)

**(3)** Now, under **Incident webhook** click on **Add** and paste the previosuly copied Squadcast Webhook URL. Similarly, under **Monitor webhook** click on **Add** and paste the previosuly copied Squadcast Webhook URL

![](images/better_uptime_4.png)

That's it, you are good to go! Your Better Uptime integration is now complete. 

Whenever Better Uptime fires Incident/Monitor alert, an incident will be created in Squadcast for it. Also, when an alert is resolved in Better Uptime, the corresponding incident gets **auto-resolved** in Squadcast.
